Customer Churn Analysis

×
PowerBI Project

Project Overview

In today’s competitive market, understanding why customers leave is key to sustained success. This project leverages advanced analytics and machine learning to analyze customer data—spanning demographics, geography, payments, and services—to uncover patterns of churn. By identifying at-risk customers and the factors driving their decisions, we deliver actionable insights that empower businesses to boost retention and strengthen loyalty.



Project Goals

  • Data Visualization & Analysis:

    Explore customer data across demographics, geography, payment/account details, and services.

  • Churner Profile Identification:

    Analyze churner characteristics to target marketing campaigns effectively.

  • Churn Prediction:

    Develop a predictive model to identify customers at risk of leaving.

Key Insights

  • Churn Rate: Identified a 12% monthly churn rate.
  • Behavioral Trends: Frequent churn among customers with high service costs.
  • Demographic Impact:
    • Age Groups: Younger customers show higher churn rates.
    • Geography: Urban customers churn less than rural customers.
  • Actionable Recommendations:
    • Offer loyalty rewards to reduce churn.
    • Analyze pricing models to retain cost-sensitive customers.
